Prime H-Line Single Stage Steam Turbines

Horizontally split

Designed in the USA for stationary applications as mechanical drivers or electric power turbine generator packages with power range capabilities up to 3MW of power.

  • Dual exhaust ports for ease of customer piping connection
  • Custom suspension system to reduce overall vibration which lowers turbine maintenance cost and increases performance
